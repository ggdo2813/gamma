## Gamma vs. The Competition

**Gamma vs. Canva**  
Canva has way more templates and design flexibility. Gamma has better AI content generation. Pick Canva for design-heavy work, Gamma for speed.

**Gamma vs. Beautiful.ai**  
Beautiful.ai focuses heavily on design intelligence and template-based layouts. Gamma is faster for generating content from scratch. Beautiful.ai is better if you want guaranteed professional-looking slides.

**Gamma vs. PowerPoint + AI add-ons**  
Tools like Plus AI work inside PowerPoint/Google Slides, so there's no export hassle. Gamma is a separate platform with its own ecosystem. If you're married to Microsoft Office, stick with PowerPoint add-ons.
